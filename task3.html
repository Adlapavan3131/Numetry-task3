About us

At Libro, we're passionate about connecting readers with the books they love. Our user-friendly platform makes it easy to discover new titles and dive into captivating stories. We believe in the power of knowledge and the joy of reading. Join us in our mission to make literature accessible to everyone. Latest Reads

Fiction

The Mystery of Shadows

There are multiple matches for fiction and the mystery of shadows, including The Shadows by Alex North and Shadows of Truth by Kevin Shief.

Thriller

Chasing Midnight

Chasing Midnight by Randy Wayne White is the 19th book in the Doc Ford series.The book is about a group of armed men who take control of an island and threaten to kill the hostages one by one. The twins in the book take over the island to free sturgeon fingerlings and crash a dolphin resort. The book is suspenseful, but some say the storyline isn't captivating.

Love

Love in Bloom

Love in Bloom is a book by Sheila Roberts about a flower shop owner who helps others through her floral arrangements. The book follows Hope Walker, the owner of Changing Seasons Floral, as she meets women who impact her perspective on her life and love.

Fantasy

Realm of Enchantment

Realms of Enchantment is a collection of fantasy tales that takes readers to a world of magic and wonder. The story follows Elara, a young girl who finds an enchanted mirror in her grandmother's attic. The story takes place in the hidden realm of Lumina, where dreams and reality intertwine.
